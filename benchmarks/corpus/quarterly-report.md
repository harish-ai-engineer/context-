# Q2 2026 Quarterly Report

This report summarizes Aurora Systems' performance for the second quarter of fiscal year 2026, covering April through June.

## Financial Highlights

Revenue grew 12 percent year over year to 48.3 million USD, driven primarily by expansion in the enterprise segment.

Gross margin improved to 71 percent, up from 68 percent in the prior quarter, reflecting infrastructure cost reductions.

### Revenue by Segment

Enterprise contracts contributed 29.1 million USD while self-service subscriptions accounted for 19.2 million USD.

## Operational Update

Headcount reached 412 employees at quarter end, with 38 net new hires concentrated in engineering and customer success.

- Launched the Aurora Edge deployment option in May.

- Achieved SOC 2 Type II certification in June.

- Opened the Singapore regional office.

## Outlook

Management expects third quarter revenue between 50 and 52 million USD, assuming stable enterprise renewal rates.
