# Refund Policy

Customers may request a full refund within 30 days of purchase. Refunds are processed to the original payment method within five business days of approval.

## Eligibility

To be eligible, the product must be unused and the request must include the original order number.

- Annual plans: prorated refund after 30 days.

- Monthly plans: no partial-month refunds.

## Exceptions

Digital goods marked as final sale are excluded from this policy, as are purchases made through third-party resellers.
